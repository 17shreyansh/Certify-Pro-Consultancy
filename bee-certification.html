BEE Certification

Bureau of Energy Efficiency Certification for Energy-Efficient Products

What is BEE Certification?

The BEE Certification involves the labelling of electrical appliances and equipment with a star rating, indicating their energy efficiency. The higher the number of stars, the more energy-efficient the product is considered.

This labelling system helps consumers make informed choices about the energy efficiency of appliances and encourages the use of energy-efficient technologies. The certification covers a wide range of electrical products, including refrigerators, air conditioners, washing machines, lighting products, and more. Manufacturers need to comply with the energy performance standards set by BEE, and their products are then tested and rated accordingly.

Why BEE Certification is Important for a Product

Consumer Awareness

A BEE-rating certificate provides consumers with valuable information about the energy efficiency of a product.

Marketability

Enhances marketability and competitiveness.

Regulatory Compliance

Ensures compliance with regulatory standards.

Government Incentives

Products with BEE-rating certification may qualify for Government incentives, providing additional financial benefits for manufacturers and consumers alike.

Environmental Impact

Demonstrates a commitment to environmental sustainability.

Brand Reputation

Boosts brand reputation and customer loyalty.

Long-term Cost Savings

Energy-efficient products typically have lower operating costs over their lifetime due to reduced energy consumption.
