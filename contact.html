Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the certification process take?

+

The timeline varies depending on the type of certification. Generally, BIS registration takes 15-30 days, GST registration takes 3-7 days, and MSME registration takes 7-15 days. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

What documents are required for certification?

+

Document requirements vary by certification type. Common documents include company registration certificate, PAN card, address proof, and specific technical documents. We provide a complete checklist after understanding your requirements.

Do you provide post-certification support?

+

Yes, we provide comprehensive post-certification support including compliance monitoring, renewal reminders, and ongoing consultation to ensure your business remains compliant with all regulations.

What are your service charges?

+

Our charges are competitive and transparent with no hidden costs. Pricing varies based on the complexity and type of certification. We provide detailed quotations after understanding your specific requirements.
