Wireless Planning and Coordination licensing for telecom equipment

What is WPC License?

Wireless Planning and Coordination (WPC) license is mandatory for manufacturing, importing, or selling wireless equipment in India. It ensures electromagnetic compatibility and prevents interference with other wireless services.

WPC certification is essential for devices like mobile phones, WiFi routers, Bluetooth devices, and other wireless communication equipment to be legally sold in the Indian market.
